Our Journey

Greetings from The Outer Clove! We are a passionate culinary adventure that started in 2005 with the goal of creating an amazing dining experience. Our restaurant began as a modest family-run business, founded in the center of Kansas City by Chef Michael Johnson and his wife, Emily. With a background in fine dining and a passion for cuisines from throughout the globe, Chef Michael had an idea for a restaurant where taste and creativity would combine to enhance the eating experience.
